Traditional methods often encounter great difficulty in spending a lot of time to determine the exact location of a missing person.

Rugged Terrain

The process of searching for and locating missing persons requires speed and accuracy, especially with complex and difficult-to-access terrain, rapid deployment, and flexibility become decisive factors in the success of any rescue effort.

Solution

SkyHelper uses wifi detection from personal devices to help locate missing people accurately and quickly, such as smartphones, smartwatches, headphones, etc. The system includes an embedded computer integrated on the UAV and Android mobile software. It operates in many different environments and terrains and supports many automation features for easy deployment.

Approach

Use of drones

Access to hard-to-reach areas: UAVs are capable of flying into hard-to-reach or dangerous areas, forests, mountains, or areas with traffic congestion. Making it easier to access and monitor difficult areas.

Autonomous flight capability: UAVs can be programmed to fly autonomously along a specific route, reducing the need for piloting skills and increasing accuracy in search missions over a pre-determined range.

Wifi detection algorithm

When users turn on wifi, the devices continuously emit wifi waves to search for wifi networks within range. The system will collect information and use wifi wave detection algorithms to determine the exact location of the missing person. Helps reduce dependence on search forces and increase the effectiveness of rescue work.

The popularity of smartphones and other smart devices such as smartwatches and wireless headphones has created a huge resource that we can tap into to solve the search and rescue problem.

Use Gemini as a virtual assistant

To support users, the system collects important data including speed, wind direction, distance, location, Wi-Fi signal strength, and live images from the UAV. All of this data is sent to Gemini, acting as a virtual assistant, which analyzes and provides recommendations on the approach and guides the next steps for handling the situation.

Responsible AI: The data collected from users includes only the MAC address and signal strength, ensuring privacy while Gemini's recommendations are accurate and transparent. SkyHelper provides precise information to rescue teams, protects personal data, and uses AI ethically.

faq

What makes SkyHelper stand out from other methods of finding missing women on the market?
  • Products on the market often have pre-installed cameras that only help to directly capture images or videos to the base, which is inconvenient when it cannot collect other parameters or most importantly, the location of the missing person.
  • Sky Helper locates the missing person's exact location including location, coordinates, altitude, and live images.
Is the system easy to use and easy to deploy on a large scale?

Yes, SkyHelper supports automatic flight with preset flight path setting, users just need to select 3 coordinates in the area suspected of missing person and the device can continue to operate automatically.

Is there any confusion when searching for someone by personal device, for example, a smartphone locates a place, but the person is lost or not there?

SkyHelper is equipped with an additional camera module including 1 infrared thermal imaging module to check the temperature at the suspected point, 1 infrared module to help operate at night, with additional camera support to minimize confusion.

What is the operating range of the device?

Flight range 10000m2, flight ceiling 500m, maximum number of found devices 500 devices, error +- 2m - +-5m, helps to maximize the chance of accurate and successful search, compared to current methods.
